These layers are redistributed here for reproducibility and remain under the
license of their original providers. Consult the source before reuse:

- `districts.geojson`, `metro-stations-in-riyadh-...geojson`,

  City / Riyadh municipality open data). They are provided under those open-data

## Restricted data — not distributed

Per-district population figures for Riyadh (GASTAT) are legally restricted and
are **not** included in this repository in any form. See the README section
"Data availability and reproducibility limits."
